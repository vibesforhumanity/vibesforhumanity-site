Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 8, 2025

Our Commitment

At Vibes for Humanity, we believe privacy is a fundamental right. We design our apps with privacy at the core.

Information We Don't Collect

Our apps, including Tetrahedron, do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • No user accounts or sign-ins required
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No third-party advertising networks
  • No behavioral data collection
  • No location tracking
  • No device identifiers

Data Storage

Any app preferences or settings you configure (such as shape selection, color choices, or haptic preferences) are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Haptic Feedback

Our apps use your device's haptic engine to provide tactile feedback. This functionality is processed entirely on-device and does not collect or transmit any data.

Audio Features

Apps that include optional ambient audio play sound files stored locally within the app. No audio data is recorded, collected, or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services in our apps.

Children's Privacy

Our apps do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. All functionality is self-contained and privacy-preserving.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our apps after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
